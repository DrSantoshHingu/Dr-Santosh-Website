  • Dr. Santosh Hingu has completed his education of MBBS<\/strong> and post-graduation in orthopedic surgery from the renowned BJ Medical College<\/strong> situated in Ahmedabad, affiliated with one of Asia’s largest healthcare hospitals, New Civil Hospital Ahmedabad. After his education, he did fellowships in joint replacement surgeries in Ahmedabad, Vadodara, and Hyderabad.<\/li>
  • Then he worked as an assistant and chief replacement surgeon in various hospitals in Ahmedabad and Rajasthan.<\/li>
  • Then he returned to his home town of Surat, and since 2020, he has been working as a consultant orthopaedic surgeon in Surat. And serving people who required joint replacement, arthroscopy surgery, or trauma surgery.<\/li>
  • Dr. Hingu has more than 7 years of experience in orthopaedic surgery and is currently attached to and working with various hospitals in Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh.<\/li>
